About Beginning Gallery SDG NTU

I would be the CHANGE, the CHANGE starts from me and you.

My heart fills up with joy and fulfilment when I'm gardening. Being able to see plants that I've cultivated develop and bear fruit gives me a sense of accomplishment unlike anything else. Gardening is my haven from the hustle and bustle of life, where I discover peace and mindfulness. My relationship with nature and my appreciation of its wonders are profound. In addition, gardening has introduced me to a remarkable network of people who share my enthusiasm. Together, we share advice, stories, and seeds while creating enduring bonds of friendship. The pleasure of savouring the flavours of recently gathered vegetables is, in the end, a reward that nourishes both the body and the soul. I hope that by sharing the joy of gardening, I will inspire others to embark on this beautiful path of growth and connection with nature.

My Vision

In my ideal future, no one will go hungry, communities will be fed, and food insecurity will no longer exist.

My Mission

My goal is to inspire and teach people about sustainable gardening techniques via compassion and empowerment, giving them the information and tools they need to grow their own wholesome food. By working together, we can combat food insecurity, encourage self-sufficiency, and build a more resilient and sustainable future for everyone.

My Goal

By enabling people and communities to grow their own food, I hope to help the SDG 2030 goal of eradicating hunger and advancing sustainable agriculture.

How it Started

The simple act of cultivating groundnuts in my neighbor's flower pot marked the start of my adventure. I carefully tended the plant, watched it grow, and ultimately saw the rewards of my labour. The delight of sharing the harvest with my neighbours made the experience nothing short of breathtaking, and it sparked something inside of me.

This enthusiasm, this feeling of companionship and accomplishment, was what kept me motivated to go on. My perception of the power of gardening and its effects on people and communities was changed by that modest endeavour. My route to passion, purpose, and the burning desire to change the world via the cultivation of nature's wealth was paved by a humble beginning.

Keeping Up

The drive to overcome obstacles and adjust to unfamiliar settings is what fuels my motivation. I was unable to find my favourite local foods after moving to another country, so I decided to start my own garden despite the bad weather. It became an opportunity to learn and master gardening in different seasons.

Despite encountering failures along the way, like my seedlings not germinating, I persevered. I was able to grow a wide variety of crops by starting seedlings indoors and moving them outdoors, including tomatoes, peppers, spinach, okra, garlic, maize, cowpea, sweet potatoes, radish, carrots, mint, lettuce, and more. The images below demonstrate the results of my labour and serve as a daily reminder of my determination and the strength of self-reliance.
